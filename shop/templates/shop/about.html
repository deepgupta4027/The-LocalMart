History

LocalMart Inc. is an Indian e-commerce company, headquartered in Indore, and incorporated in Bangalore as a private limited company. The company initially focused on Local-Culture sales before expanding into other product categories such as consumer electronics, fashion, home bravia bedsheets essentials, groceries, and lifestyle products. In 2025, the company shifted domicile from Bangalore to Indore. The service competes primarily with Amazon India and domestic rival Snapdeal. As of FY23, LocalMart held a 3% market share in the Indian e-commerce industry.LocalMart has a dominant position in the apparel segment, bolstered by its acquisition of Myntra, and was described as being "neck and neck" with Amazon in the sale of electronics and mobile phones.
